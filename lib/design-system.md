# Design System Documentation

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- Used for primary actions, links, and brand elements
- Range: 50 (lightest) to 900 (darkest)
- Main: 600 (#0284c7)

### Secondary Colors
- Used for secondary actions and accents
- Range: 50 (lightest) to 900 (darkest)
- Main: 600 (#9333ea)

### Neutral Colors
- Used for text, backgrounds, and borders
- Range: 50 (lightest) to 900 (darkest)
